Our Story

Donkey Dumpster was founded by our Lead Donkey, Ryan Gale - the first person to graduate from THE Ohio State University with a degree in Entrepreneurship.

Before Donkey Dumpster, Ryan owned Premier Valet Parking and Premier Festival Solutions. When COVID hit, mass gatherings were shut down, effectively closing Premier Festival Solutions. Then restaurants closed, ending Premier Valet Parking. For the first time in his life, Ryan found himself divorced, living in an RV, and without a source of income.

The turning point came when Ryan's friend Tommy called for help cutting down a tree. After a long day of work, Tommy's wife Brooke asked, "You guys aren't just going to leave the wood there?" When Ryan suggested they could burn it over time, Brooke simply said, "I think it needs to go."

That moment sparked an idea. When someone came the next day to haul the wood away for $400, Ryan's entrepreneurial mind went to work. He realized his box truck could handle similar jobs. Quickly, he:

  • Filed an LLC
  • Secured insurance and workers' compensation
  • Created what we believe is THE BEST logo of all time

Just like that, Ryan was back in business - this time with an essential needs service! Today, Donkey Dumpster operates with 4 trucks and 6 trailers, ready to travel anywhere in Ohio to "Hee Haul" your junk away!
